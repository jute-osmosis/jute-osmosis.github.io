Asians in America Support and Professional Network

AASPN is a pan-Asian and professional organization.

Our Mission

Asians in the Americas Support and Professional Network (AASPN) is a registered non-for-profit civil organization primarily engaged in advancing Asian-American interests across Canada and the US. The goal of AASPN is to better connect Asians and Asian Americans with their local communities, as well as to provide effective programming for networking and professional advancement. AASPN is the spiritual successor of several grassroots organizations aimed at addressing racial barriers Asian Americans face in the workplace and beyond.

Dim Sum Round Table

Every month we host a Dim Sum lunch where we discuss issues that young Asians face today. We socialize, and share our experiences over good food and tea.
